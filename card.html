Bossy Cat

The average cat sleeps 16-18 hours per day.Cats cannot see in complete darkness, only at low light levels.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 46 cms
  • Average Lifespan: 4-5 years
  • Habitat: forests, deserts and urban

Humans greet each other by shaking hands; cats greet one another by touching their noses together.
